Our Coffee

Latte

A milk coffee that is a made up of one or two shots of espresso, steamed milk and a final, thin layer of frothed milk on top

Macchiato

An espresso coffee drink, topped with a small amount of foamed or steamed milk to allow the taste of the espresso to still shine through

Cappuccino

A hot coffee drink made with espresso, steamed milk, and milk foam, layered on top of each other. The perfect balance of espresso, steamed milk and foam

Espresso Shot

A concentrated, full-flavored coffee beverage that's made from forcing pressurized water through finely-ground coffee beans.
